feedpress

Pressing together all your RSS news thats fit to press.

  • Supports rss feed standards
  • Creates typst output files.

Structs§

  • BiblioEntry 🔒
    The bibliographic information that links to a ContentEntry record and this conforms to the standard found in the hayagriva crate and project.
  • ContentEntry 🔒
    A specific item of content used by the layout engine to create a section of news or content in the final PDF. Think of this like an “article” of sorts.
  • FeedConfig 🔒
    Contains our application configuration. Configuration is written in the TOML format, seen most places.
  • FeedEntry 🔒
    Holds detailed information about a specific source RSS feed to pull articles from. Configuration is held in the [[feed]] array in the configuration toml.
  • Press 🔒
    A container to hold all of our compiled ContentEntry items.
